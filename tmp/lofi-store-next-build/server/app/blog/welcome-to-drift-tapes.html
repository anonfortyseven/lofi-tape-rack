Welcome to Drift Tapes

Introducing our new blog—a space for stories, artist spotlights, and everything lofi.

By Drift Tapes Team•February 5, 2025

Welcome to the Drift Tapes blog! We're excited to launch this new space where we'll share stories from behind the scenes, spotlight incredible artists, and dive deep into the world of lofi music.

What to Expect

Here's what we'll be covering:

  • Artist Spotlights — Get to know the producers behind your favorite beats
  • Production Tips — Learn techniques from the masters of the lofi sound
  • Behind the Scenes — See how we curate our catalog and run the tape shop
  • Community Stories — Hear from collectors and listeners like you

Why We Started This

"Music is the shorthand of emotion." — Leo Tolstoy

At Drift Tapes, we believe lofi is more than background music. It's a feeling, a community, a lifestyle. This blog is our way of celebrating that culture and connecting with you on a deeper level.

Stay Connected

The best way to stay updated is to check back here regularly. We'll be posting new content every week—from quick tips to in-depth features.

Thanks for being part of the Drift Tapes family. We can't wait to share more with you.

— The Drift Tapes Team
