About

Café Morena is the duo of siblings Lucas and Marina Costa, who grew up in their parents' boteco in Vila Madalena, São Paulo's bohemian quarter. Their father played bossa nova guitar, their mother sang—and every Sunday afternoon was an impromptu jam session with neighborhood musicians. When Lucas discovered beat-making software in college, he immediately knew what he wanted to do: preserve that Sunday afternoon feeling forever. Marina's breathy vocals, sung in Portuguese, anchor tracks that blend their father's João Gilberto records with head-nodding beats. Their music has become synonymous with the 'coffee shop lofi' aesthetic, and they've partnered with numerous Brazilian café chains. They still perform Sunday sessions at their parents' bar.

Signature Sound

Nylon guitar melodies and soft Portuguese vocals over swinging bossa rhythms with tape warmth and café ambiance

Visual Aesthetic

Tropical modernism. Bold, flat colors inspired by Brazilian mid-century design. Teal, coral, sunny yellow, palm green. Illustrations of coffee cups, tropical plants, beach umbrellas. Hand-drawn typography with a vintage travel poster feel.
